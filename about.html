Our History

The Faculty of Medicine was established in 1963 and has since been at the forefront of medical training and research in Zimbabwe and Southern Africa. The faculty collaborates with hospitals, research institutions, and global partners to enhance healthcare delivery and education.

Our Mission

To provide quality education and research that produces world-class medical professionals equipped to meet the health needs of society.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in medical education, research, and innovation in Africa.
